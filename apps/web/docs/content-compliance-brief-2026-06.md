## 1. Brand positioning (say this, not that)

| Always say | Never say |
|---|---|
| "ASVAB prep" / "test prep" / "study tool" | "military recruitment tool" / "join the military" |
| "Unlock career options" / "see what jobs your score qualifies you for" | "Enlist now" / "sign up for the military" |
| "Free score calculator" / "free practice questions" | "Free ASVAB" (the test itself isn't ours) |
| "ASVAB-style questions" / "practice questions" | "Real ASVAB questions" (the CAT-ASVAB is not publicly released) |
| "Eligibility varies by branch, line scores, openings, and medical/background" | Absolute guarantees about job qualification |
| "ASVAB Hero" (brand name, always capitalized) | "ASVABHero" / "Asvab hero" / "asvab hero" |

**Frame:** ASVAB Hero is an **education / exam-prep brand**. We help people understand and prepare for a standardized test. We are not a recruiting tool. This framing clears ad policy on every platform and avoids weapons/combat-content flags.

## 2. Approved hook formats (ranked by proven pull)

### Hook 1: "Your score → these jobs" reveal
- Pick an AFQT or line score, show what it unlocks per branch.
- On-screen caveat (required): *"Eligibility varies by branch, line scores, openings, and medical/background."*
- CTA: "Calculate yours — link in bio"

### Hook 2: Live AFQT calculation / "rate my score"
- Type 4 line scores into the calculator → live percentile + verdict.
- Great for duet/stitch "calculate mine" UGC.
- CTA: "What's YOUR score? Free calculator — link in bio"

### Hook 3: "Can you answer this?" speed challenge
- Countdown timer + answer reveal + brief explanation.
- Use ASVAB Hero's original practice questions ONLY.
- Never claim "real ASVAB question" — the test is not publicly released.
- CTA: "4,500+ practice questions free — link in bio"

### Hook 4: Myth-bust (softened)
- Use: "What recruiters don't explain about your ASVAB score" / "the adaptive-test myth" / "AFQT vs line scores"
- Do NOT use: "Your recruiter is lying" (high attention, low trust, ad-review risk).
- CTA: "Know your real score — link in bio"

### Hook 5: Score transformation story
- "I went from 32 to 87 — here's the plan"
- Anchor the daily-study-loop as the mechanism.
- Works creator-led and as student-testimonial UGC.
- CTA: "Start your plan — link in bio"

### Cross-cutting angle
"Pass the ASVAB even if you're 'not a test person'" — weave reassurance into hooks 3 and 4. This over-indexes with the anxious 16-20 audience.

## 5. Compliance rules (non-negotiable)

### FTC disclosure (sponsored/paid content)
- **All** sponsored, gifted, or compensated creator content must include clear disclosure.
- Use: `#ad` or `#sponsored` at the START of the caption (not buried at the end).
- On video: verbal disclosure within the first 5 seconds ("partnered with ASVAB Hero") OR persistent text overlay.
- This is non-negotiable. The audience is heavily under-18; FTC enforcement on teen-targeted content is active.

### Creator eligibility
| Eligible | NOT eligible |
|---|---|
| Veterans (any branch, any era) | Active-duty service members |
| Civilians / students / aspiring enlistees | Military recruiters (active) |
| JROTC cadets (with parental consent if minor) | DoD civilian employees in official capacity |
| Guard/Reserve members (off-duty, personal capacity, with unit PAO clearance) | Anyone representing themselves as acting on behalf of the U.S. military |

**Why:** The Joint Ethics Regulation (DoD 5500.07-R) bars active-duty members and recruiters from endorsing commercial products. Violation exposes the creator to UCMJ action and ASVAB Hero to reputational risk.

**Screening question for every creator:** "Are you currently active-duty or serving as a military recruiter?" If yes → politely decline, explain why.

### Content that is banned
- Claiming ASVAB Hero questions are "real ASVAB questions" (the CAT-ASVAB is controlled)
- Guaranteeing specific test scores, job placements, or enlistment outcomes
- Military recruitment messaging (we are test prep, not a recruiting tool)
- Weapons, combat footage, or glorification of violence
- Disparaging any military branch, recruiter, or the ASVAB program itself
- Using official DoD logos, seals, or insignia without authorization
- Any content targeting children under 13

### Under-18 paid advertising
- Meta, TikTok, Google, and Reddit all restrict paid targeting of users under 18 to age + geo only.
- Reddit cannot target under-18 at all.
- Do NOT attempt to performance-target 16-17 year olds on paid social. Target 18-20 + parents instead.
- Put paid budget in **search** (Apple Search Ads, Google Search), not social.
